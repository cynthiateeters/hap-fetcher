Meet Fetcher!

I took my robot phone to the shelter and learned that Fetcher, being a Shih Tzu and Bichon Frise mix, was my perfect pal. All that breed research — fetching, exploring responses, caching lookups, converting weights from kg to lbs — led me right here. I named this little one Fetcher, because I had to learn the developer way to fetch before I found my new best friend. 🟠
